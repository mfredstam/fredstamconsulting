Services

Specialised expertise across the full embedded software lifecycle.

  • Software Engineering & Development

    Embedded and real-time software development in C and C++, leveraging RTOS expertise to deliver deterministic, resource-efficient systems. From bare-metal drivers to complex multi-threaded applications — designed for reliability and long-term maintainability.

  • Safety-Critical Software

    Development and assurance of software under IEC 61508, DO-178C, and ISO 26262 across Aerospace, Defense, and Automotive domains. Rigorous process adherence, traceability, and verification to meet the highest integrity levels.

  • Cyber Security

    Security engineering for embedded and connected systems — threat modeling (STRIDE/TARA), secure coding practices, and vulnerability analysis. Fredstam Consulting helps teams build resilience into products from the ground up, aligned with IEC 62443 and automotive UNECE R155.
